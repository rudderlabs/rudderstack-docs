# Warehouse Actions

RudderStack's **Warehouse Actions** feature lets you use the enriched customer data residing in your data warehouse and route it to your entire data stack, including analytics, sales, and marketing tools.

<div class="successBlock">

With this feature, you can set up your data warehouse as a source in the <a href="https://app.rudderstack.com/" target="_blank">RudderStack dashboard</a>, choose the data to import, and then sync it to your desired destination.
</div>
