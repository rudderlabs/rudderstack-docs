[HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/) is a leading marketing and sales platform that helps you track leads as well as inbound marketing and sales.

RudderStack supports sending data to HubSpot via HubSpot's [legacy](https://legacydocs.hubspot.com/docs/overview?_ga=2.34803302.670362313.1663315856-97001172.1658910392) and [new](https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/overview) APIs.
