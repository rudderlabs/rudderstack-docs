[Rockerbox](https://www.rockerbox.com/) provides multi-touch attribution by centralizing all your marketing spend and campaigns. It helps companies measure the impact of their marketing activities while optimizing the media expense.

RudderStack supports Rockerbox as a destination to which you can seamlessly send your customer data.
