# Google Ads 

A developer token from Google lets your app connect to the Google Ads API. To retrieve your developer token, sign in to your manager account. You must be signed-in to a Google Ads manager account before continuing.

https://ads.google.com/home/tools/manager-accounts

Navigate to TOOLS & SETTINGS > SETUP > API Center. This option appears only for Google Ads manager accounts.

For more information on obtaining the dev token: https://developers.google.com/google-ads/api/docs/first-call/dev-token


ID: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/1704344?hl=en
